Our Story

How we started and our journey to becoming Dammam's trusted handyman service

Since 2018

Fanni Pro Dammam was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to provide reliable, affordable, and professional handyman services to homes and businesses in Dammam. What started as a small team of three skilled technicians has grown into one of the most trusted handyman services in the Eastern Province.

Our founder, Usman Ali, recognized the need for a professional and reliable handyman service in Dammam. With over 10 years of experience in construction and home maintenance, he assembled a team of skilled professionals who share his commitment to quality workmanship and customer satisfaction.
